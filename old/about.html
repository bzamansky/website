About Me

Hi there. My name is Batya Zamansky, as you probably know by now.

I am a student at Cornell University in the College of Arts and Sciences, class of 2017. I am currently undeclared, but I am leaning towards Computer Science or Information Science for my major. I enjoy computer science mainly because it allows me to build cool stuff (like this website!) and solve problems in ways I might not think of otherwise.

When not coding my website or doing copious amounts of homework, I enjoy singing with the Cornell Chai Notes and writing fiction. I love to knit and crochet, and spend lots of my free time crocheting turtles for my friends.
